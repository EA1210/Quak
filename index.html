This is the Quak Empire


Rules of The Empire:
1. All citizens have the right to protest, but the government also has the right to retaliate against protest
2. All crimes against The Empire are punishable either by banishment or another punishment elected by the council
3. Citizens have the right to overthrow their leader at any time, so long as they can provide compelling evidence to support it so that the other citizens may vote on whether they accept the new ruler or if the current ruler shall continue to rule
4. Any and all rules are subject to change
